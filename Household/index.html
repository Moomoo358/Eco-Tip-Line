Household Tips

A main way to be eco-friendly within the household is to switch from plastic bags to resuable bags. The plastic bags that you use at the store for your produce are too flimsy to be recylcyed properly. By investing in resuable bags you stray away from throwing out all the plastic bags you would have gotten from the store. Since many people tend to grab a good amount when it comes to bagging their produce. You put your apples in one, your cilantro in another, and before you know it you have over 10 of those flimsy bags in your cart. And you're know you are going to throw in the trash when you get home and clean your produce. Hence, save yourself the trouble and get that resuable bag!
